BlockchainUNN is a campus club of Blockchain and cryptocurrency Enthusiasts within and outside the University community.they Educates people on the importantance of blockchain Technology and how to leverage in the space and also provide opportunities to earn in the space.

COMMUNITY LOGO

To represent the lion UNN holds initial esteem as well as Blockchain Concept, the logo was scripted from scratch to combine both the lion and Blockchain nodes.

THE COMMUNITY VISION AND MISSION

To ensure mass Adoption of the knowledge of Blockchain and crypto to both lecturers and students and how to leverage in it. we have an aim of becoming the best campus Blockchain community in Africa and in the world at large.

FIRST EVER BLOCKCHAIN UNN CAMPUS ONLINE MEET UP

To further spread the Blockchain education, the first online webinar kicking off the establishment was held on Sunday, 17th October, 2011. Over 32+ students were present and educated at this webinar.
